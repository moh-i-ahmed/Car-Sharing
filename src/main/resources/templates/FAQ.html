Overview

Car Sharing or carsharing is a model of car rental where people rent cars for short periods of time. It is designed as an extension of the transportation network.

This application simulates the car sharing model in the city of Leicester, UK. This application allows a user to pick up a car at one location and drive it to a drop-off location. You sign up and login to access the service, add a payment card in Wallet and you're ready to go.

Here's information on how to use this car sharing service.

Using the service
Step 1

Ensure you have verified your account using the link in the registration email. If your account is verified, proceed to Step 3, otherwise take a moment to verify your account.

Step 2

If the link has expired, you can send another by navigating to Profile, then click more and "Send Verification Email".

Step 3

Navigate to "Wallet" and add a valid payment card.

Step 4

Navigate to "Find a Car" and enter the pickup & drop-off locations & the times of your request.

Rules

Car usage times are between 30 minutes and 24 hours.

Requests must be made at least 30 minutes before the desired start time. This is to ensure the car is ready to be used.

Step 1

Go to the pickup location by clicking the Show Directions button which will automatically open directions in Google Maps.

Step 2

Identify the car using the registration number and description.

After following the steps of "How to find the car?"

Step 1

You can unlock the car by clicking the Unlock button one minute before your requested start time.

Step 2

Type your access code into the number pad on the car handle to enter the car.

Step 3

Drive safely 🙂

Once you arrive at the drop-off location, ensure you have all your personal belongings, simply exit the car and click the End button.
This will lock the car and complete your request.

If you don't end the request, it will end automatically after the requested ending time has passed and you will have 15 seconds to park and exit the car before it is locked automatically.
Base Charge: £5 is the minimum request charge.

Request Pricing: This is calculated as the base charge, plus 30p times request duration, plus 20p times the distance covered.

Cancellation Fee: £8 for canceling a request. If you cancel a request within 10 minutes of the start time, you will be charged for full amount for the request.

Damage Fee: This will be assessed based on the extent of the damage.
Wallet contains all your payment methods. Here, you can see, add, and remove the payment cards you have linked with your account.

Add Card: You can securely and safely add your card information by clicking the Add Card button.

Remove Card: You can remove your card from your account by clicking the trash can icon .

Your account
Updating personal information: You can change your account details in the Profile page by clicking the Edit icon .

Changing your password: You can change your password by clicking More. Then click the Change Password button and enter your new password.

Closing your account: Navigate to Profile and click More. Then click the Delete Account button and follow the prompts.
